Game of Life

What is Game of Life

Game of life is a cellular automata created by John Horton Conway, an English mathematician. It is a zero player game, which means the outcome is decided by the initial state. Initial state can be randomized, or customized by clicking on cells to flip their state.

Rules

1. When a cell is dead, but has exactly 3 alive neigbors out of 9, it becomes alive 2. When an alive cell has exactly 2 or 3 alive neigbors, it becomes alive, otherwise it becomes dead.
